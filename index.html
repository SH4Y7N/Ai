01 — Basics

What is AI?

Artificial Intelligence, or AI, is when a computer can think and learn like a human.

AI can analyze information. It can find patterns. Then it can make decisions. It does not get tired. It does not need to sleep. It can learning from data.

You already use AI every day. Your phone, your maps app, and your music app all use AI. It is not science fiction anymore. It is part of normal life.

02 — Timeline

History of AI

AI did not appear overnight. It grew step by step, over many years.

1956

Birth of AI

Scientists met at Dartmouth College. They used the words "Artificial Intelligence" for the first time. This meeting started it all.

1997

Deep Blue Defeats Kasparov

IBM's computer, Deep Blue, beat the world chess champion, Garry Kasparov. It showed that computers could solve problem and beat humans at games.

2011

Siri and Alexa

Voice assistants entered our homes and phones. Suddenly, we could talk to AI and it talked back.

2022+

ChatGPT and Modern AI

New AI tools can write, chat, and create images. AI became a tool for everyone, not just scientists.

03 — Categories

Types of AI

Not all AI is the same. We can group it into three types.

Simple AI

Weak AI

This AI can do only one job. For example, a chess app or a face filter. Most AI today is Narrow AI.

General AI

Strong AI

This AI could do any task a human can do. It could learn new things by itself. It does not exist yet.

05 — Balance

Advantages & Disadvantages

Like any tool, AI has good sides and bad sides.

✅ Advantages

  • It saves time on boring tasks.
  • It works fast, all day and night.
  • It helps doctors and scientists.
  • It makes fewer small mistakes.
  • It gives people new tools to create.

❌ Disadvantages

  • It can take away some jobs.
  • It can share wrong information.
  • It can copy human bias.
  • It raises privacy questions.
  • It needs a lot of energy to run.

08 — Summary

The Future is Here

Artificial Intelligence has grown from a simple idea in 1956 into a technology that is part of our daily lives. It helps us work, learn, communicate, and create new ideas. At the same time, it brings challenges such as privacy and job changes. AI will continue to improve, so learning about it today will help us in the future.
